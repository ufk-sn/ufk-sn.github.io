Welcome to my Website!

I graduated from Zonguldak Karaelmas University, TR with a bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering in 2013. I received my Master’s degree in Structural Engineering from Louisiana State University in August 2018.

During my graduate studies, I focused on the seismic response of concrete gravity dams. In my thesis, the fragility curves of concrete gravity dam were obtained to make a risk assessment, providing me with a comprehensive understanding of dam behavior under earthquake loads.

After graduation from LSU, I have had five years of valuable experience working for the Department of Dams and Hydropower Plants in the State Hydraulic Works of Turkey (DSI). My role here has involved structural and hydraulic design of spillways and outlet works, allowing me to develop skills in creating functional and cost-effective designs aligned with project requirements.
